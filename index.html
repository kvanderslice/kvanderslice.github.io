Hello, I'm Kristina a front end developer currently at Fifty and Fifty, working on projects for clients including Qualcomm, Susan G. Komen, ICRC, LifeStraw, Wildcoast, United Way Bay Area and PSI.

As a web engineer with Fifty and Fifty I've had the opportunity to development 25+ websites, microsites and landing pages for clients in the nonprofit and social good community. I have a passion for building creative and usable websites and am constantly looking to grow in my skillset.


I'm currently utilizing HTML, CSS/SCSS, JavaScript, jQuery, GSAP, mySQL, Git, Gulp, WordPress, PHP and Shopify in my work. I am studying React and UX/UI in my free time.

My interest in web development also spans to my time outside of work, as I am a club faciliator with Girls Who Code, teaching young women skills in computer science and sisterhood as well as a gold project advisor for Girls Scouts where I am working with a Girl Scout to build resources for women interested in learning computer science and coding. Prior to my current career I was a full time photographer and now use that skill to volunteer with Make-A-Wish Foundation as a wish and event photographer.
