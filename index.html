Hello, I'm Kristina a Web Developer, Graphic Designer, Photographer, and Instructor. Currently with Cordial, overseeing design and development as part of the Marketing team. Previously with Fifty and Fifty, where I worked as a developer on websites for clients including Qualcomm, Susan G. Komen, ICRC, LifeStraw, Wildcoast, United Way Bay Area and PSI.

Combining skills in graphic design, web development, and project management I am able to deliver on the technical and creative needs of projects while effectively managing the scope, timeline, and requirements. In my time as a web engineer with Fifty and Fifty I had the opportunity to development 25+ websites, microsites and landing pages for clients in the nonprofit and social good community, and transferred what I learned there to building creative and usable sites as a freelancer.

My interest in web development also spans to my time outside of work, as an instructor teaching web development and CMS at UCSD Extension, a club faciliator with Girls Who Code, teaching young women skills in computer science and sisterhood and a gold project advisor for Girls Scouts, working with a Girl Scout to build resources for girls interested in learning computer science and coding. Prior to my current career I was a full time photographer and now use that skill to volunteer with Make-A-Wish Foundation as a wish and event photographer.
